How it works

Three steps. That's it.

1

Install the extension

One click from the Chrome Web Store. No account required to get started.

2

Pin notes on any page

Right-click or use the toolbar to drop a note. Drag it anywhere, pick a color, and start typing.

3

Come back anytime

Your notes are right where you left them. Revisit the page and they're waiting for you.
